Menstrual Cups

What is a menstrual cup?

A small plastic or silicone device that fits inside of the vagina to catch menstratuion. It's a great alternative to pads and tampons. If you are into reducing waste, reducing the introduction of chemicals a cup may be a good option for you! So many colors, shapes and options, there is something for everyone.

Style and size

There are many options of cups based on your cervix height and preference. There are bell shaped, cones, and different stem according to your preference.

Pros

  • Reduce waste
  • Reduce chemicals
  • Cost-effective

Cons

  • If not wearing correctly, can cause discomfort
  • UTI or bladder infection
  • Messy
